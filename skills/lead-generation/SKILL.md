# 🎯 Outbound Lead Gen & Prospecting Engine

## 🗺️ Ontological Map
**Target Segment** → **Trigger Event** → **Value Prop** → **Bridge** → **Ask (Meeting)**

---

## 📥 Inputs & 📤 Outputs

### Inputs
- **Offer:** What are we selling?
- **Niche/Market:** Industry, size, geography.
- **Triggers:** "Just hired a CMO", "Raised Series A", "Low SEO score".

### Outputs
- **Prospecting Scripts:** Email, LinkedIn, or Cold Call.
- **Sequencing Plan:** Day 1, Day 3, Day 7, Day 14 cadence.
- **Lead Quality Matrix:** Scoring system (1-10).

## Instructions

1. **Find the Trigger:** Outreach without a reason is spam. Use context.
2. **Personalization v2.0:** Don't just placeholder `{{first_name}}`. Personalize the *reason* for contacting.
3. **The Multi-Touch Bridge:** LinkedIn like → LinkedIn comment → Email.
