# 📧 Email Marketing & Relationship Nurturing

## 🗺️ Ontological Map
**Enrollment** → **Value/Story** → **Trust Building** → **Offer/Conversion**

---

## 📥 Inputs & 📤 Outputs

### Inputs
- **Current Subscriber State:** Where did they come from?
- **Goal:** Click a link? Buy a product? Fill a form?
- **Tone:** Personal, Corporate, or Hype.

### Outputs
- **Subject Lines:** 3 variations with predicted open-rate logic.
- **Copy:** Conversational content with clear CTAs.
- **Sequence Plan:** Timing and dependency mapping.

---

## Instructions

1. **The Subject Line Hook:** Use curiosity or urgency without being clickbaity.
2. **Mobile-First Copy:** Short paragraphs, high white space.
3. **The "P.S." Strategy:** Use the end of the email for additional CTAs or secondary hooks.
