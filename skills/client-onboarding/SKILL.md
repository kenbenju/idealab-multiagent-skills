# 🤝 Client Onboarding & Customer Success

## 🗺️ Ontological Map
**Welcome** → **Orientation** → **Setup** → **First Win**

---

## 📥 Inputs & 📤 Outputs

### Inputs
- **Project Scope:** What was sold.
- **Contact Details:** Key stakeholders.
- **Account Requirements:** Tools, credentials, or data needed.

### Outputs
- **Welcome Packet:** PDF or email with next steps.
- **Kickoff Agenda:** 30-min meeting structure.
- **Access List:** Everything the team needs from the client.

---

## Instructions

1. **Reduce Post-Purchase Dissonance:** Gratitude and immediate value.
2. **Set Expectations:** Communication channels and meeting rhythm.
3. **Automate the Friction:** Checklists for passwords and assets.
