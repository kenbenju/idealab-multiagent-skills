# 🔍 SEO Architecture & Search Intent Content

## 🗺️ Ontological Map
**Keyword** → **Search Intent** → **Topic Clustering** → **Content Structuring** → **Link Logic**

---

## 📥 Inputs & 📤 Outputs

### Inputs
- **Primary Keywords:** What do we want to rank for?
- **Competitor URLs:** Who currently holds the top spot?
- **Target Audience:** Searching for info or ready to buy?

### Outputs
- **Content Brief:** H1, H2s, keyword density, and internal link plan.
- **Optimized Copy:** Writing for humans *and* algorithms.
- **Meta Data:** Titles and descriptions (max 60/160 chars).

---

## Instructions

1. **Solve for Intent:** Don't just write. Answer the user's question specifically.
2. **E-E-A-T Compliance:** Add Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trust.
3. **Internal Linking:** Every piece of content must link to the next stage in the funnel.
