# 📋 Business Proposals & High-Ticket Closing

## 🗺️ Ontological Map
**Problem Discovery** → **Solution Mapping** → **Benefit/ROI** → **Social Proof** → **Closing/Price**

---

## 📥 Inputs & 📤 Outputs

### Inputs
- **Customer Pain Points:** What keeps them up at night?
- **Our Solution:** Key features and benefits.
- **Expected ROI:** Tangible or intangible growth.

### Outputs
- **Proposal Document:** Professional and persuasive layout.
- **Pricing Strategy:** Anchor, Standard, and Premium tiers.

---

## Instructions

1. **Agitate the Problem:** Show them you understand their pain.
2. **Paint the Future:** The version of their business with your solution.
3. **The ROI Calculator:** Show them how the proposal pays for itself.
